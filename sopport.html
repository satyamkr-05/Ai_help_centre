How to Use

A first-time setup walkthrough
01

Create your workspace

Sign up with your work email and give your workspace a name. This becomes your team's home base.

02

Invite your team

Go to Settings → Members and enter teammate emails. They'll get an invite link that expires in 7 days.

03

Create your first board

Click New Board from the sidebar, choose a template or start blank, and add your first few tasks.

04

Automate the busywork

Open Automations to set rules like "move to Done when checklist is complete," no code required.
