Human Resource (HR) Policy

Welcome to the Human Resource (HR) Policy section. This document outlines the company's policies, procedures, and employee responsibilities. Every employee is expected to understand and follow these policies to maintain a productive, professional, and respectful work environment.

1. Working Hours

Standard office hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Employees are expected to arrive on time and maintain regular attendance.

  • Office Timing: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Lunch Break: 1 Hour
  • Weekly Off: Saturday & Sunday

2. Attendance Policy

Employees must mark attendance daily using the company attendance system. Late arrivals and unauthorized absences may affect performance evaluation.

  • Attendance must be marked every day.
  • Three consecutive absences without notice may result in disciplinary action.
  • Employees should inform their manager before taking leave.

3. Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to various types of leave based on company guidelines.

Leave Type Days Per Year
Casual Leave 12 Days
Sick Leave 10 Days
Earned Leave 15 Days

4. Work From Home Policy

Employees may work from home for up to two days per week with prior approval from their reporting manager. Availability during office hours is mandatory.

  • Manager approval required.
  • Employees must attend online meetings.
  • Daily work updates must be submitted.

5. Salary Policy

Salaries are credited on the last working day of every month directly into the employee's registered bank account.

  • Salary Slip available through Employee Portal.
  • Income Tax deductions follow government rules.
  • Annual performance appraisal conducted once every year.

6. Code of Conduct

Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.

  • Respect colleagues and clients.
  • Maintain confidentiality of company information.
  • Avoid harassment and discrimination.
  • Follow ethical business practices.
  • Protect company assets and resources.

7. Dress Code

Employees are expected to wear neat and professional attire during office hours.

  • Business Casual: Monday – Thursday
  • Casual Dress: Friday
  • Formal Dress during client meetings.

8. Health & Safety

The company is committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace.

  • Follow emergency evacuation procedures.
  • Report unsafe conditions immediately.
  • Maintain workplace cleanliness.
  • Follow IT security and cybersecurity guidelines.

9. IT & Security Policy

Employees must use company devices responsibly and protect confidential company information.

  • Do not share passwords.
  • Use strong authentication.
  • Avoid installing unauthorized software.
  • Report phishing emails immediately.

10. Performance Evaluation

Employee performance is evaluated annually based on productivity, teamwork, innovation, attendance, and overall contribution to company goals.

11. Employee Benefits

  • Health Insurance
  • Provident Fund (PF)
  • Paid Annual Leave
  • Performance Bonus
  • Employee Training Programs
  • Career Development Opportunities
